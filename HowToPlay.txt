--- THE BOARD --
A 3x3 grid where each cell contains its own smaller 3x3 grid.
            
--- HOW TO MOVE ---
1. The first player chooses any cell to start.
2. Your move 'sends' the opponent to the outer cell that matches your inner cell choice.
3. Winning an inner grid claims that entire outer cell.
            
--- RULES ---
• First player is decided by a coin toss.
• If you are sent to an outer cell that is already won or full, you can move ANYWHERE.
